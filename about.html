Our Story

Hi, I'm Michael Lamothe, and EverAfterPicks is more than just a wedding inspiration site—it's a love letter to my fiancée, Kailey.

We recently got engaged, and I couldn’t be more excited to start this journey with her. Kailey used to be a wedding photographer, capturing the magic of other couples’ big days. Now she’s a teacher, pouring her heart into the classroom. She’s creative, compassionate, and deserves a wedding that reflects everything she’s given to others.

I created EverAfterPicks as a way to share the beautiful, rustic, and romantic finds I’ve discovered while planning our own wedding. It’s also a way to support our dream—through affiliate links, we may earn a small commission when you shop through the site. Every bit helps us build the wedding Kailey has always imagined, and we’re so grateful for your support.

Whether you're planning your own celebration or just love timeless wedding style, I hope this space feels like a cozy corner of the internet where inspiration meets intention.

Thanks for being here, and for being part of our story.

With love,
Michael Lamothe
